Do you know your typing speed?

Scroll down to find out!

Haven't you ever wanted to know your typing speed? As a secretary, it's almost do or die to have a fast typing speed and for a programmers, well we want to look like we're actually doing something am I right? Well, even if you want to know your typing speed for fun you're at the right place! You can even check your Phone typing speed here! Amazing right? Well there are 13 different texts with different symbols and letters. You dont have to type all of them, however you will get a more accurate idea of your typing speed if you do 5 tests and above. You can restart at anytime, however you will have to start from the first test. I had immense fun coding this and I hope you enjoy checking your typing speed. If you spot any bug contact me here.
